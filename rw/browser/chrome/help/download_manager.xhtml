Mucungibyimurwa ni iki?

Mucungibyimurwa ni ikiranga cya &brandShortName; gituma ushobora gushaka no gucunga ibyimurwa dosiye byawe bya kera n'ubu. Ni uburyo buboneye bwo kwirinda igerekerana ry'amadirishya y'ibyimurwa atandukanye ariko nako bukomeza gutuma hagaragara umubare w'ibyimurwa.

Mucungibyimurwa izagaragara igihe ukuruye dosiye, igihe utoranyije Ibikoresho > Ibyimurwa, n'igihe usunitse &accelKey;+J&accelKey;+Y.

Ni iki nshobora gukoresha Mucungibyimurwa?

Usibye kugumisha ibyo wimuye byose ahantu hamwe, Mucungibyimurwa ituma ibikorwa biboneye by'iyimurwa bishobora kugerwaho kuri buri kintu wimuye.

Gusubika
Ushobora gusubika iyimura ribonetse ryose ririmo gukorwa ukanda ihuza ryo Gusubika riri iruhande rw'icyinjizwa mu dosiye. Ibi bishobora kugira akamaro, uregero iyo ukeneye gufungura icyimurwa gitoya cyatangiwe nyuma y'icyimurwa kinini. Gusubika ibyimurwa bituma ushobora guhitamo mu byimurwa byawe ibifite akamaro kurusha ibindi. Iyo ushaka gukomeza gukurura izo dosiye, ukoresha gusa ihuza ryo Gusubikura kugira ngo urangize gukurura ibyo byimurwa.
Kureka
Iyo nyuma yo gutangira gukurura usanze utagikeneye iyo dosiye, kureka iyimura biroroshye: koresha gusa ihuza ryo Kureka riri iruhande rw'icyinjizwa mu dosiye kugira ngo ukireke kandi hanaboneke umwanya mu mwinjiro wawe kugira ngo ushobore gushakisha no gukurura ibindi byimurwa.
Gufungura Dosiye
Iyo gukurura byarangiye, ihuza ryo Gufungura rizagaragara hafi y'icyinjizwa mu dosiye. Rikoreshe kugira ngo ufungure dosiye.
Gukuraho Icyinjizwa mu Dosiye
Niba udashaka kubika amakuru mu cyimurwa cyihariye, kuyavanamo bisaba gusa gukanda n'imbeba. Koresha gusa ihuza ryo Kuvanaho riri iruhande rw'icyinjizwa mu dosiye kugira ngo uyasibe.
Kongera Kugerageza Gukurura
Iyo hagize impamvu ituma gukurura bitarangira, biroroshye kugerageza kongera gukurura. Kugira ngo wongere kugerageza gukurura icyanze, koresha gusa ihuza ryo Kongera Kugerageza rikurikira icyinjizwa mu dosiye noneho &brandShortName; izongera igutangirize ikurura.
Kwerekana Ububiko bw'Ibikururwa
Niba waboneje &brandShortName; kugira ngo ibike ibyimurwa byo mu bubiko bwo mu Mucungibyimurwa &pref.pluralCaps;, ushobora gufungura ubu bubiko ukanze Izinabubiko rihera idirishya rya Mucungibyimurwa.

Mucungibyimurwa &pref.pluralCaps;

&pref.plural; bigenewe Mucungibyimurwa bigerwaho hafunguwe umwanya w'Ibymurwa mu &pref.pluralCaps; idirishya. bituma ushobora guhindura ibikururwa byinshi &pref.plural;. Andi makuru yerekeye ibyimurwa bitandukanye &pref.plural; aboneka mu &pref.pluralCaps; paji.
